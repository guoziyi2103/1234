Important Tips

重要提示

Courier Service

快递服务

Our front desk accepts courier packages for all guests. Please provide your room number and name when sending or receiving packages. We will keep your packages safe until pickup.

酒店前台可为所有住客代收快递包裹，收发快递时请提供房间号和姓名，我们会妥善保管包裹直至您领取。

Laundry Room (3rd Floor)

洗衣房（3楼）

The self-service laundry room on the 3rd floor is equipped with washing machines and dryers. Our staff can help collect, deliver, wash and dry your laundry upon request (free of charge).

3楼自助洗衣房配备洗衣机和烘干机，如有需要，工作人员可协助收取、送回及清洗烘干衣物（免费服务）。

Room Bedding Purchase

房间床品购买

If you like the hotel's bedding (pillows, quilts, bed sheets), you can purchase them at the front desk. We offer the same high-quality bedding used in the rooms for sale.

如果您觉得我们酒店的床品（枕头、被子、床单等）不错的话，可以前往前台进行购买，我们提供与客房同款的高品质床品售卖。

Atour Tea Giving (Public Welfare Project)

亚朵奉茶（公益项目）

Atour Tea Giving is a public welfare initiative where we provide free hot tea to outdoor workers (cleaners, delivery riders, taxi drivers) year-round. This represents our commitment to social responsibility and care for the community.

亚朵奉茶是一项公益项目，我们全年为户外工作者（环卫工人、外卖骑手、出租车司机等）提供免费热茶，体现酒店的社会责任与人文关怀。

Late Night Service & Supper

深夜服务与夜宵

Our 24-hour front desk provides thoughtful late-night services. Complimentary supper is available from 21:30 PM to 23:00 PM at the Ninth Floor Restaurant for all guests, including porridge with 4 kinds of pickled vegetables, 2 kinds of homemade side dishes, and 2 kinds of steamed dim sums.

酒店前台提供24小时贴心服务，每晚21:30至23:00在9楼餐厅为住客提供免费夜宵，包含粥品搭配4种配粥酱菜、2种自制小菜，以及粥品和2种蒸点。

Room Refrigerator Items

房间冰箱物品

All mineral water and beverages in the room refrigerator are complimentary (free of charge). Note: Only Jimu rooms are equipped with refrigerators.

房间冰箱内的矿泉水和饮料皆是免费提供的。注：只有几木房型才配备有冰箱。

WiFi Password

WiFi密码

The high-speed WiFi password for hotel rooms and public areas is the last 6 digits of your passport number (for foreign guests) or ID card number (for Chinese guests).

酒店客房及公共区域的高速WiFi密码为您的护照后六位（外籍宾客）或身份证后六位（中国籍宾客）。

Public Transport

公共交通

天府广场地铁站（1/2号线）- 距酒店300米（步行3分钟），直达成都东站（20分钟）、春熙路（5分钟）、锦里古街（15分钟）。人民东路公交站（100米）有10余条公交线路。
